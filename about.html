HELLO ☄ —

I’m Kara, a UX Designer and recent Masters grad from the University of Michigan where I studied Human Computer Interaction.

I believe that design is a way to methodically re-think and define what is possible. I look forward to starting my career where I can envision solutions that have a timeless impact.

Last summer I interned on the product design team at PTC. I currently design at the MI2 Lab, a U of M research group focusing on next generation interface research while simultaneously seeking out my next big career move.

Offline you can find me zipping around Ann Arbor in my purple roller skates or tending to my absurd number of plants.
